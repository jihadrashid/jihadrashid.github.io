About me

I have been working as a Research Consultant at the Remote Sensing Division of the Center for Environmental and Geographic Information Services (CEGIS) since May 2020. My role involves conducting research and analysis related to remote sensing technology and its applications in environmental and geographic information services. I am also involved in developing and implementing projects related to remote sensing and environmental monitoring.

Prior to my work as a Research Consultant, I worked as a Research Assistant at the Department of Geography and Environmental Studies under Dr. Md. Atiqur Rahaman. My responsibilities included assisting with research projects, conducting data analysis, and contributing to academic publications.

I have also completed BSc & MS degree in Geography and Environmental Studies from the University of Chittagong, which has provided me with a strong academic background in the field.

Overall, my experience and education suggest that I have a solid foundation in the field of Geography and Environmental Studies, with a focus on remote sensing technology and its applications.
